SkiaViewer Public API Surface Area Baseline
============================================

Types:
  StrokeCap (RequireQualifiedAccess DU)
    | Butt | Round | Square

  StrokeJoin (RequireQualifiedAccess DU)
    | Miter | Round | Bevel

  BlendMode (RequireQualifiedAccess DU)
    | Clear | Src | Dst | SrcOver | DstOver
    | SrcIn | DstIn | SrcOut | DstOut
    | SrcATop | DstATop | Xor | Plus | Modulate
    | Screen | Overlay | Darken | Lighten
    | ColorDodge | ColorBurn | HardLight | SoftLight
    | Difference | Exclusion | Multiply
    | Hue | Saturation | Color | Luminosity

  TileMode (RequireQualifiedAccess DU)
    | Clamp | Repeat | Mirror | Decal

  FontSlant (RequireQualifiedAccess DU)
    | Upright | Italic | Oblique

  FontSpec (Record)
    Family: string
    Weight: int
    Slant: FontSlant
    Width: int

  PathDirection (RequireQualifiedAccess DU)
    | Clockwise | CounterClockwise

  PathCommand (RequireQualifiedAccess DU)
    | MoveTo | LineTo | QuadTo | CubicTo | ArcTo | Close
    | AddRect | AddCircle | AddOval | AddRoundRect

  Shader (RequireQualifiedAccess DU)
    | LinearGradient | RadialGradient | SweepGradient | TwoPointConicalGradient
    | PerlinNoiseFractalNoise | PerlinNoiseTurbulence | SolidColor | Image | Compose
    | RuntimeEffect

  TrimMode (RequireQualifiedAccess DU)
    | Normal | Inverted

  Path1DStyle (RequireQualifiedAccess DU)
    | Translate | Rotate | Morph

  PathEffect (RequireQualifiedAccess DU)
    | Dash | Corner | Trim | Path1D | Compose | Sum

  HighContrastInvertStyle (RequireQualifiedAccess DU)
    | NoInvert | InvertBrightness | InvertLightness

  ColorFilter (RequireQualifiedAccess DU)
    | BlendMode | ColorMatrix | Compose | HighContrast | Lighting | LumaColor

  BlurStyle (RequireQualifiedAccess DU)
    | Normal | Solid | Outer | Inner

  MaskFilter (RequireQualifiedAccess DU)
    | Blur

  ColorChannel (RequireQualifiedAccess DU)
    | R | G | B | A

  ImageFilter (RequireQualifiedAccess DU)
    | Blur | DropShadow | Dilate | Erode | Offset | WithColorFilter
    | Compose | Merge | DisplacementMap | MatrixConvolution

  ClipOperation (RequireQualifiedAccess DU)
    | Intersect | Difference

  Clip (RequireQualifiedAccess DU)
    | Rect | Path | Region

  PointMode (RequireQualifiedAccess DU)
    | Points | Lines | Polygon

  VertexMode (RequireQualifiedAccess DU)
    | Triangles | TriangleStrip | TriangleFan

  PathOp (RequireQualifiedAccess DU)
    | Difference | Intersect | Union | Xor | ReverseDifference

  PathFillType (RequireQualifiedAccess DU)
    | Winding | EvenOdd | InverseWinding | InverseEvenOdd

  RegionOp (RequireQualifiedAccess DU)
    | Difference | Intersect | Union | Xor | ReverseDifference | Replace

  Transform3D (RequireQualifiedAccess DU)
    | RotateX | RotateY | RotateZ | Translate | Camera | Compose

  Paint (Record)
    Fill: SKColor option
    Stroke: SKColor option
    StrokeWidth: float32
    Opacity: float32
    IsAntialias: bool
    StrokeCap: StrokeCap
    StrokeJoin: StrokeJoin
    StrokeMiter: float32
    BlendMode: BlendMode
    Shader: Shader option
    ColorFilter: ColorFilter option
    MaskFilter: MaskFilter option
    ImageFilter: ImageFilter option
    PathEffect: PathEffect option
    Font: FontSpec option

  Transform (RequireQualifiedAccess DU)
    | Translate | Rotate | Scale | Matrix | Compose | Perspective

  Element (RequireQualifiedAccess DU)
    | Rect | Ellipse | Line | Text | Image | Path | Group
    | Points | Vertices | Arc | Picture | TextBlob

  Scene (Record)
    BackgroundColor: SKColor
    Elements: Element list

  InputEvent (RequireQualifiedAccess DU)
    | KeyDown | KeyUp | MouseMove | MouseDown | MouseUp
    | MouseScroll | WindowResize | FrameTick

  Backend (RequireQualifiedAccess DU)
    | Vulkan | GL | Raster

  ImageFormat (RequireQualifiedAccess DU)
    | Png | Jpeg

  ViewerConfig (Record)
    Title: string
    Width: int
    Height: int
    TargetFps: int
    ClearColor: SKColor
    PreferredBackend: Backend option

  ViewerHandle (Sealed Class)
    implements IDisposable
    member Screenshot: folder: string * ?format: ImageFormat -> Result<string, string>
